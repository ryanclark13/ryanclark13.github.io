Diet

Honey Badgers are carnivores, but their diet is extremely wide. They will eat small food items like insects, scorpions, lizards, and birds but the size of their prey does not scare them. They will eat small crocodiles, pythons, cobras, and black mambas. Also, they will eat springhares, polecats, and antelope. Locating their prey comes easy because of their acute sense of smell and their digging, climbing, and swimming abilities. As their name suggests, they will go to great lengths to raid honeybee hives in search of bee brood.

Honey Badger Doesn't Care

Please don't ever approach a honey badger.
